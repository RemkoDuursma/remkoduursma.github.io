Why R?

  • R has rapidly become one of the leading tools for data science. When you need more control and flexibility in your analysis and visualizations than the usual point-and-click programs can offer, R is the best alternative.
  • Because R is the standard for statisticians, basically any statistical analysis is implemented and available in R.
  • R is ranked the 8th most used programming language in the world, with over 2 million users. This wide usership means you can find lots of help when you need it.
  • In my experience, most newcomers to R who have never written code before can quickly learn how to use R.
  • With R/Shiny, you can quickly develop powerful interactive apps and dashboards, without learning HTML, CSS or Javascript.
  • Did I mention it is free?
